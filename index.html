Our Mission

Making AI work for humans.

Most organisations have an AI ethics statement. Very few have operational governance that holds up under scrutiny. No existing certification framework starts from the intersection of psychology, technology, and human behaviour. With the EU AI Act entering enforcement on 2 August 2026, the gap between good intentions and genuine compliance is about to become a material risk.

The Ambler Institute exists to close that gap.
