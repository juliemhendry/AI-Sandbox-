Our Mission

Most organisations have an AI ethics statement. Very few have operational governance that holds up under scrutiny. With the EU AI Act entering enforcement in August 2026, the gap between good intentions and genuine compliance is about to become a regulatory, reputational, and financial risk.

The Ambler Institute exists to close that gap.

What We Do

Advisory

We help organisations move from AI ethics statements to operational governance. Our work starts with understanding where you actually are: how mature your AI governance is, where the gaps sit, and what needs to happen before enforcement. We work at board level because that is where accountability lives.

Certification

We are developing a certification framework grounded in the intersection of technology, psychology, and human behaviour. Built by practitioners who have implemented governance in real organisations, not theorists who have written about it from universities.

The Concordat

The Concordat for Ethical AI is the strategic advisory community that shaped the Institute's direction. Inaugurated on 11 May 2026 at the Worshipful Company of Information Technologists, it brings together leaders from technology, psychology, law, faith, education, and industry to ensure AI governance reflects the full complexity of human experience.
